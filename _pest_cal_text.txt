India Pest & Disease Calendar — 102 Pests
Crop, Peak Season, Weather Trigger, Damage — Point-form Reference
1. Rice Leaf Roller
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jul–Sep)
Weather: Temp 25–30°C, RH >80%, humid cloudy weather
Damage: Larvae roll leaves into tubes; feed inside; white/brownish streaks on leaves
2. Rice Leaf Caterpillar
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jul–Oct)
Weather: Warm humid conditions, RH >75%
Damage: Skeletonise leaves; defoliation in heavy infestations
3. Paddy Stem Maggot
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jul–Aug), early transplanting
Weather: Temp 25–28°C, high humidity post-transplant
Damage: Maggots enter tiller base; cause deadheart and whiteear
4. Asiatic Rice Borer
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif & Rabi; peaks Jul–Aug
Weather: Temp 26–30°C, RH >80%
Damage: Deadheart in vegetative stage; whiteear at heading; worst in Kharif
5. Yellow Rice Borer
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jun–Sep)
Weather: Warm humid; temp 27–32°C
Damage: Stem tunnelling; deadheart and whiteear; major pest in coastal India
6. Rice Gall Midge
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jul–Aug), peak at tillering
Weather: Temp 25–29°C, RH >80%, early Kharif rains
Damage: Silvery shoot (onion leaf/trumpet leaf); heavy losses in eastern India
7. Rice Stemfly
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jun–Sep)
Weather: Temp 26–30°C, high humidity
Damage: Maggot mines into central shoot; deadheart at seedling/tillering stage
8. Brown Planthopper (BPH)
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Aug–Oct), late season
Weather: Temp 25–30°C, RH >80%, dense canopy
Damage: Hopper burn; direct sap sucking; transmits Grassy Stunt and Ragged Stunt viruses
9. White Backed Planthopper
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jul–Sep)
Weather: Temp 20–28°C, RH >80%
Damage: Sap sucking; hopperburn; less damaging than BPH but co-occurs
10. Small Brown Planthopper
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Aug–Sep)
Weather: High humidity, dense crop
Damage: Transmits Rice Stripe Virus; sap sucking on leaf sheaths
11. Rice Water Weevil
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jun–Aug), nursery to tillering
Weather: Flooded conditions, temp 25–30°C
Damage: Adults notch leaf margins; larvae feed on roots; stunting and yield loss
12. Rice Leafhopper
Crop: Rice | Season: Kharif (Jul–Sep)
Weather: Warm humid; temp 25–30°C
Damage: Sap sucking; hopperburn; transmits Tungro virus — critical pest
13. Grain Spreader Thrips
